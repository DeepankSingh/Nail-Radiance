About Us

Crafting Confidence
One Nail at a Time

Nestled in the heart of Dehradun, Nail Radiance is more than just a salon — it's a sanctuary. Since 2017, we've been Uttarakhand's most-trusted destination for luxury nail care, beauty treatments, and transformative experiences.

From intricate hand-painted nail art to soothing spa pedicures and bridal full-glam, our certified artists bring your vision to life using only premium, cruelty-free products in a pristine, hygienic environment.

Certified & internationally-trained nail technicians
OPI, CND, Orly & top-brand products only
Hospital-grade sterilization protocols
Relaxing, feminine, fully air-conditioned studio
